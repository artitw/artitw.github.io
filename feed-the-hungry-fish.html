Mechatronic Penny Arcade Game Objective

We are Team ABC, and we seek to design and build a Mechatronic Penny Arcade Game (MPAG) suitable for players of all ages and technical background.  The MPAG will attract and entertain players to pry as many pennies as possible from them.  In addition to the game’s real-time entertainment, Stuff Won in an Arcade Game (SWAG) will be rewarded to players who score enough points.  To meet these goals, our MPAG will involve analog user input, site and sound stimulation on the part of the user, non-contact sensing, and electromechanical action.
