Appletizer

Autumn 2006
            Concepts, models, creation
Final Product
Polycarbonate and ABS container, Stainless steel pins and blades
ARTIT WANGPERAWONG

Information

Unlike existing apple slicers, Appletizer is safer and more effective to use.  The blades remain stationary and hidden within the container while slicing.  The pusher cap takes advantage of the apple’s physiology to center the apple and keep it steady for every cut.  The stainless steel pins ensure that the apple is completely sliced.  Every apple is sliced perfectly with Appletizer.  All the materials used to make Appletizer are completely dishwasher safe and FDA approved.
Existing apple slicers:
  • Apples don’t slice all the way.
  • Difficult to center the apple, especially if apple is asymmetric.
  • Easy to contact sharp blades with hands.
  • User needs to stand over a table to slice the apple.
Appletizer:
  • Stainless steel pins ensure complete cuts.
  • Pusher cap’s center pin keeps apple stable and centered even if apple is asymmetric.
  • Blades remain stationary and hidden within the container during slice.
  • User can slice apples sitting down.
  • Appletizer is completely dishwasher safe
